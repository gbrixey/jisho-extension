#Privacy Policy

##What information does this extension collect?

- This extension collects search queries when you enter text into the search box in the toolbar.
- This extension also collects search queries when you select text on a website, right-click on the selected text and then click the "Search Jisho.org for..." option in the context menu.
- This extension does not store search queries that you have entered in the past. Search queries are discarded immediately after use.
- This extension does not collect or store any user data other than search queries.

##How does this extension use/disclose your information?

- Search queries are transmitted to Jisho.org for the purpose of displaying the Jisho.org search results page in a new browser tab.
- Search queries are not transmitted to any third party other than Jisho.org.
- Search queries are not sold to any third party.
